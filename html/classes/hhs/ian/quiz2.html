Portland, Oregon

Portland is the biggest city in oregon. It was named after Portland, Maine.
It started to become populated in the 1830s and is home to the Portland Timbers (soccer team)
and also the Portland Trailblazers (basketball team). more than 500,000 live in Portland, Oregon.
Portland is a beautiful city with many things to do and many things to look at.

  • Portland Japanese Garden
